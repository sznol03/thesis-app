About Fecal Occult Blood Test


Info Pictures

    About the FOBT test:

  • Used to detect blood stool
  • Taken at home - Just send test in the mail
  • Requires 3 consecutive samples
  • Detects blood in stool
  • Used once a year
  • Costs around $2-4 dollars

Foods that you can't eat

  • Red meat
  • Cantaloupe
  • Broccoli
  • Turnip
  • Radish
  • Horseradish
  • Vitamin C ( Citrus fruits like Oranges, Grapefruit)
  • Aspirin
  • ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin)

How to interpret FOBT results

  • When test is negative (blue) - repeat test in a year
  • Postive results DO NOT mean cancer.
  • When positive, seek a colonoscopy
