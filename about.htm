About Colorectal Cancer



Information Pictures
  • Cancer of large intestine
  • Affects over 142,000 men and women over 50 a year
  • 1 in 20 develop CRC over their lifetime
  • Family history can increase risk of developing CRC
  • Many cases have no symptoms
  • Early detection is important in preventing death

Risk factors include

  1. high fat, low fiber diet
  2. Red meat diets
  3. Family history
  4. Genetics

Symptoms can include:

  1. stomach/abdominal pain
  2. blood in stool
  3. unexplained weight loss
  4. diarrhea
