About Fecal Immunohistochemical Test (FIT)


Information Pictures
  • Detects blood in stool
  • Taken at home
  • A better for detecting colorectal cancer than FOBT
  • Uses chemicals to identify blood in stool sample
  • No diet restrictions
  • Used once a year

    • Cost

  • Costs $10-18 dollars

    • Results

  • If negative result - Take test once a year
  • When positive requires follow up screening (Colonoscopy)
