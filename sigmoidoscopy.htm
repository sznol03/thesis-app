Flexible Sigmoidoscopy

Information Pictures
  • While lying down on your side, a doctor users a camera to observe your colon for anything abnormal.
  • Used once every 5 years
  • Reduces mortality by 66%
  • Does not view the entire colon

    • Cost

  • $150-$300

    • Test Day

  • Some Doctors advise drinking clear liquids only a few days before test
  • Does not require anaesthesia
  • May take up to an hour

    • Results

  • If anything abnormal is observed, doctor can remove or test for cancer
  • MAY require a follow-up colonoscopy
